About me

Pioneering Web3 innovation and education across Africa.

With over six years immersed in the blockchain and Web3 space, my career is dedicated to building innovative technology and empowering Africa's next generation of tech talent. My journey is fueled by a fascination with decentralization's potential, leading me to master Ethereum and Solidity.

I believe technology alone isn't enough; education is the catalyst for mass adoption. This conviction drove me to co-found Web3 Africa, creating a pan-African ecosystem for builders, and to establish Lynx Web3 Academy for high-quality developer education.

As a GaiaNet AI ambassador, I'm at the nexus of decentralized systems and artificial intelligence, advocating for a future where technology is powerful, equitable, and accessible to all.
