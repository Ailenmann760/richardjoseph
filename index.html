About Me

Pioneering Web3 Innovation

With over six years immersed in the blockchain and Web3 space, my career is dedicated to building robust decentralized systems and empowering Africa's next generation of tech talent.

I believe technology alone isn't enough; education is the catalyst for mass adoption. This conviction drove me to co-found Web3 Africa, creating a pan-African ecosystem for builders.

As a strategic partner with GaiaNet AI, I operate at the intersection of decentralized systems and artificial intelligence, advocating for infrastructure that is powerful, equitable, and accessible.
