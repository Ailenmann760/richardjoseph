About Me

Pioneering the future of decentralization and digital empowerment across Africa.

With over six years deep in the blockchain and Web3 space, I've dedicated my career to not just building innovative technology but also to educating and empowering the next wave of talent in Africa.

My journey began with a fascination for the potential of decentralization. This led me to master Ethereum and Solidity, developing robust smart contracts and full-stack decentralized applications. But I quickly realized that technology alone isn't enough; education is the key to mass adoption.

Driven by this mission, I co-founded Web3 Africa to create a pan-African ecosystem for builders and creators. I also founded Lynx Web3 Academy to provide structured, high-quality education for aspiring developers and enthusiasts.

As an ambassador for GaiaNet AI, I am at the forefront of merging decentralized systems with artificial intelligence, advocating for a future where technology is both powerful and equitably distributed. My goal is to create tangible opportunities and foster a self-sustaining digital economy across the continent.
