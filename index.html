Introduction

Overview.

With over six years deep in the blockchain and Web3 space, I've dedicated my career to not just building innovative technology but also to educating and empowering the next wave of talent in Africa.

My journey began with a fascination for decentralization, leading me to master Ethereum and Solidity. I develop robust smart contracts and full-stack dApps, but I believe education is the true key to mass adoption.

Driven by this mission, I co-founded Web3 Africa to create a pan-African ecosystem for builders, and founded Lynx Web3 Academy to provide high-quality education for aspiring developers.

As an ambassador for GaiaNet AI, I am at the forefront of merging decentralized systems with artificial intelligence, advocating for a future where technology is both powerful and equitably distributed across the continent.
